This android application was created to assist with a previous job role. I found it time consuming to continue looking through the vast table on paper to find the specific type of equipment by looking for a bar-code pattern.

Simply scan the bar-code and it will search it's database, returning the type of STB for my paperwork. This vastly improved productivity.
